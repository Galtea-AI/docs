OTel Tracing, New Models, and UX Improvements

2026-01-19

Standardized Tracing with OpenTelemetry

Galtea Traces have been upgraded to use the OpenTelemetry (OTel) standard. This update aligns our tracing capabilities with industry standards, enabling easier future integrations with your existing monitoring tools. Read more about traces.

New Models Available

We have expanded the selection of models available for evaluating your products. You can now leverage:

  • Claude-Sonnet-4.5
  • GPT-5 Family (GPT-5.2, GPT-5.1, GPT-5, GPT-5-mini)

Revamped Onboarding & View Modes

We've overhauled the onboarding experience with clearer code paths for Development vs. Production use cases.

Additionally, you can now toggle the platform UI between Development and Production modes. This hides irrelevant features to keep your workspace focused.

General Improvements

  • Enhanced Forms: Standardized and improved forms across the platform for better usability.

Enjoy the improvements!

Coming Next

We're continuing to improve the core experience:

  • Test-Agnostic Metrics: A new metric type that works seamlessly across all test types and production evaluations.

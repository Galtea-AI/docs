Single-Turn Evaluations & Bulk Actions

2026-02-02

Evaluate Single-Turn Interactions

You can now run evaluations on specific single turns (even when directly creating an Inference Result), rather than being restricted to evaluating full threads/sessions. This granularity allows for precise analysis of individual exchanges within a larger conversation.

Bulk Actions in Tables

Managing your data is faster than ever. You can now select multiple rows in dashboard tables to perform actions in bulk, such as deleting multiple Test Cases or Sessions simultaneously.

Stability & Performance

  • Metric Stability: We've improved the consistency and reliability of several core metrics.

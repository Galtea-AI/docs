## Full Visibility with Agent Tracing

You can now add **[Traces](/concepts/product/version/session/trace)** to your [Sessions](/concepts/product/version/session) and [Inference Results](/concepts/product/version/session/inference-result). This unlocks deep observability into your AI agents, allowing you to understand exactly what they are doing at each step of executionâ€”not just the final output.

Traces capture internal operations such as:
- **Tool Calls:** API requests, calculations, or data fetching.
- **Retrieval Steps:** Vector database searches and RAG context retrieval.
- **Chain Orchestration:** Internal routing and decision-making logic.
- **LLM Invocations:** Prompts sent to underlying models.

With the new **[Trace Service](/sdk/api/trace/service)** in the SDK, you can visualize the full execution tree, debug errors, analyze latency for specific operations, and audit agent behavior with granular detail.
